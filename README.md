# 🍬 US Candy Distribution Sales & Logistics Analytics
This project analyzes sales performance, product profitability, factory operations, and customer distribution for a US candy distributor using Power BI.
## 📌 Project Overview

The **Candy Distribution Sales & Logistics Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze sales performance, product profitability, customer demand, factory performance, and geographic sales trends for a US candy distributor.

The dashboard transforms raw business data into meaningful insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor KPIs, identify high-performing products, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance across products and regions.
- Identify top-performing and low-performing products.
- Measure profitability using profit and profit margin.
- Evaluate factory performance based on sales contribution.
- Understand customer demand across different states.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

## 📊 Key Insights

- Chocolate is the highest-performing product division, contributing approximately **₹131.7K** in sales.
- Other and Sugar divisions contribute only a small percentage of total sales.
- The top-selling products include:
  - Wonka Bar – Triple Dazzle Caramel
  - Wonka Bar – Scrumdiddlyumptious
  - Wonka Bar – Milk Chocolate
- California records the highest sales, followed by New York and Texas.
- Lot's O' Nuts is the highest-performing factory.
- Sales are concentrated within a few high-performing products and regions.
